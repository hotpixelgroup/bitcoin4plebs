# Code of Conduct

This project teaches strangers to verify things for themselves. That only works in a community where questions are safe to ask.

**Expected:**

- Be kind and patient, especially with beginners. "Newbie questions" are the entire audience of this site.
- Argue about ideas, code, and evidence, never about people.
- Assume good faith. A wrong claim gets a citation, not a pile-on.
- Stay on topic: how Bitcoin works. Price talk, shilling, and financial advice do not belong here.

**Not tolerated:**

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language of any kind.
- Trolling, spam, or sustained disruption of issues and pull requests.
- Publishing others' private information.

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, issues, and PRs that violate this, and may block repeat offenders. To report a problem, email harry@hotpixelgroup.com.

This applies in all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and commits.
